About us

Who we are

Founded in 2015 in Colombo, Sri Lanka, Nuclei Technologies is a boutique software services company built on the belief that startups and growing businesses deserve the same engineering rigour as Fortune 500 enterprises.

Co-founded by Anjana Somathilake and Isuru Kodituwakku — two industry veterans with hands-on experience across the USA and Sri Lanka — Nuclei was born from a direct request by Anjana's former clients and colleagues to build a software partner that delivers with the quality of a high-end global software services company, at the agility of a boutique team.

Lean, client-obsessed, and AI-powered — so you get more, for less.

Our philosophy

We build with you, not just for you.

At Nuclei, our clients are not project tickets — they are long-term partners. We invest deeply in understanding your business, your users, and your goals before a single line of code is written.

Deep problem understanding

We take the time to understand the root of your challenge — not just the surface requirement. Every engagement starts with listening, not pitching.

Relentless focus on outcomes

We measure success by the impact our software creates for your business — not just delivery milestones. If something isn't working, we say so and we fix it.

Long-term partnership

Our average client stays with us for 2–3 years. Trust built through consistent delivery, honest communication, and genuine care for our clients' success.

Our methodology

AI-assisted SDLC — more value, lower cost

We go beyond AI tooling. Our delivery methodology is built on spec-driven development and the BMAD framework — ensuring every project is deeply planned, architected, and validated before a single line of code is written. AI is embedded at every phase.

B

Blueprint

AI-assisted requirements gathering, user story mapping & full spec definition before any build begins.

M

Model

Domain modelling, data architecture & API contract design — validated with AI before development starts.

A

Architect

Solution design, technology decisions & non-functional requirements — with AI-reviewed architecture decisions.

D

Develop

AI-accelerated build, automated testing, code review & deployment — reducing manual effort at every step.

The result for our clients: faster time-to-market, significantly reduced development costs, fewer post-launch defects, and full visibility at every stage — from first spec to final deployment.
